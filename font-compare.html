All text is Poppins. Only numbers, speeds, IPs, and percentages use the monospace candidate. Notice how each mono changes the "feel" of the data: JetBrains/Fira feel technical, IBM Plex feels neutral/professional, Space Mono adds character, and Inconsolata almost disappears into the layout. Pick based on whether you want numbers to stand out (Space Mono, JetBrains) or blend in (Inconsolata, IBM Plex).
